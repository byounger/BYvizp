Rural vs Urban Population


STATIC

by Brittney Younger

I developed this visualization for a course at Maryland Institute College of Art (MICA). The assignment was to develop a story around a designated dataset. After cleaning and analyzing the dataset in Excel, I decided to focus on the outliers and overall range of time by creating a back-to-back horizontal bar chart and line chart for one-on-one comparison.





Certain bars' opacity were adjusted to allow viewers to focus on the highlighted countries. I added annotation for the outliers to give the viewer additional context about the visualization's overall story. All in all, I think this was a successful piece for a viewer to learn about a country's agricultural and urban development.
